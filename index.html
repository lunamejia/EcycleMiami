Why is ewaste an issue in Miami?
25 states have already enacted legislation establishing statewide e-waste recycling programs; Florida, unfortunately, is not one of them. The Florida Department of Environmental Protection strongly encourages that unwanted electronic products be recycled. The problem is that there are no specific state laws regarding the discarding of electronic products; only very general guidelines exist. Ecycle Miami hopes to help the problems regarding electronic waste in Florida.
